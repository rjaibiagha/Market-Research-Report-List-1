<p><ul><li>Axial Jet Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Jet Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car park jet fan ventilation system market features two main types: axial jet fans and centrifugal jet fans. Axial jet fans utilize a straight airflow design, effectively moving air through long distances and suitable for larger spaces. In contrast, centrifugal jet fans employ a rotating impeller to generate high-velocity airflow, making them ideal for confined areas with limited space for installation. Both types are essential for maintaining air quality and safety in underground and multi-level car parks.</p></p>
