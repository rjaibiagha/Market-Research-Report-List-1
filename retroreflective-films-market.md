<p><p>The retroreflective films market consists of two primary types: glass bead type and micro-prism type. Glass bead type films utilize small glass beads embedded in a polymer matrix to reflect light back to its source, providing good visibility at night. Micro-prism type films, on the other hand, use microscopic prisms to achieve higher reflectivity and clarity, enhancing performance in various lighting conditions. Each type serves specific applications, from safety signage to vehicle markings, catering to diverse visibility requirements.</p></p>
