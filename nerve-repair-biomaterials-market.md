<p><p>The nerve repair biomaterials market encompasses applications in direct nerve repair, or neurorrhaphy, and nerve grafting. Neurorrhaphy involves the direct stitching of nerve ends together, using biomaterials to support healing and restore function. In contrast, nerve grafting entails the use of grafts, either autologous or synthetic, to bridge gaps between severed nerves. Both techniques utilize specialized biomaterials to enhance regeneration, reduce scar tissue formation, and promote functional recovery in patients with peripheral nerve injuries.</p></p>
