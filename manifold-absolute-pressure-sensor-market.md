<p><p>The Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) sensor market is categorized into two main types: analog sensors and digital sensors. Analog MAP sensors output a continuous voltage signal that varies with pressure changes, providing real-time data to the engine control unit. In contrast, digital MAP sensors deliver pressure readings in a binary format, offering enhanced precision and ease of integration with modern electronic systems. Both types are essential for optimizing engine performance and fuel efficiency in automotive applications.</p></p>
