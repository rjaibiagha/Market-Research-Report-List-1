<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorescent acrylic sheet market is primarily divided into two types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring molten acrylic resin into molds, resulting in a thicker, more durable product with excellent optical clarity and color vibrancy. Extruded acrylic sheets, on the other hand, are created through an extrusion process, yielding thinner, more uniform sheets that are lightweight and cost-effective. Both types are used in various applications, including signage, displays, and decorative elements.</p></p>
