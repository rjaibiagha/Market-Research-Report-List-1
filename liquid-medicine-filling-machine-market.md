<p><p>The liquid medicine filling machine market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Atmospherical Pressure Filling Machine**: Utilizes atmospheric pressure to fill containers, ideal for non-viscous liquids.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Pressure Filling Machine**: Operates under pressurized conditions to fill thicker liquids, ensuring efficiency and precision.</p><p>3. **Vacuum Filling Machine**: Employs vacuum pressure to draw liquids into containers, minimizing air exposure and oxidation, suitable for sensitive formulations. </p><p>Each type serves distinct liquid characteristics, optimizing the filling process based on the product's requirements.</p></p>
