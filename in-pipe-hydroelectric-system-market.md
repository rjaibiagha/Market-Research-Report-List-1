<p><p>The In-Pipe Hydroelectric System market consists of two main types: internal and external systems. Internal systems are designed to be installed within existing water pipelines, harnessing the kinetic energy of flowing water for electricity generation without major infrastructure changes. In contrast, external systems are situated outside the pipeline and use similar water flow, but may require additional structures or modifications to channel water effectively. Both types offer sustainable energy solutions by utilizing existing water infrastructure for power generation.</p></p>
