<p><p>Drop On Demand (DOD) inkjet printing inks are categorized based on their solvent types. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and widely used for indoor applications due to their low toxicity. Alcohol-based inks offer faster drying times and are suited for a variety of surfaces but may pose environmental concerns. MEK (Methyl Ethyl Ketone) inks provide strong adhesion and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. Additionally, various other inks may include UV-curable and solvent-based options, catering to diverse printing needs.</p></p>
