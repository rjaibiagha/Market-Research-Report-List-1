<p><ul><li>Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) Reaction</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Burners NOx Control Systems market includes two primary types: Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). SNCR utilizes reagents like ammonia or urea injected into flue gas at high temperatures to reduce nitrogen oxides without a catalyst. In contrast, SCR employs a catalyst to enhance the reaction efficiency, allowing lower temperatures and more effective NOx reduction. Both technologies aim to mitigate emissions from combustion processes, promoting environmental compliance and sustainability.</p></p>
