<p><ul><li>Standard</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood sugar meter market is primarily categorized into two types: standard meters and continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Standard meters are traditional devices that require finger pricks to measure blood glucose levels at specific intervals. In contrast, CGMs provide real-time glucose readings by using a sensor placed under the skin, offering continuous monitoring throughout the day. This allows for better management of blood sugar levels, making CGMs increasingly popular among individuals with diabetes seeking more proactive health management.</p></p>
