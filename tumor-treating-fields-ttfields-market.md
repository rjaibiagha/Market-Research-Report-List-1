The market is segmented into low frequency and intermediate frequency types. Low frequency TTFields primarily target slow-dividing tumors, providing a non-invasive option for conditions like glioblastoma. Intermediate frequency TTFields aim at a broader range of cancers, including those with faster growth rates. Both types work by interfering with the mitotic process, enhancing traditional therapies, and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
