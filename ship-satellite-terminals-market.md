<p><p>Ship satellite terminals are categorized into three types based on power output and bandwidth capabilities. High Power Block Up Terminals (BUPTs) are designed for large vessels and provide extensive bandwidth for demanding applications like high-definition broadcasting. Middle Power BUPTs offer a balanced solution suitable for medium-sized ships, delivering reliable connectivity without excessive capacity. Low Power Block Up Terminals are ideal for smaller vessels, focusing on essential communication needs and cost-efficiency while maintaining basic satellite connectivity.</p></p>
