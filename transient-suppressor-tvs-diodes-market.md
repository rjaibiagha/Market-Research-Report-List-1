<p><p>The transient suppressor TVS diode market is categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed for applications where protection is needed against positive transients, making them suitable for single-polarity systems. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes protect both positive and negative transients, making them ideal for circuits with AC voltage or dual polarity. The choice between these types depends on the specific voltage requirements and application environments, ensuring optimal protection for electronic components.</p></p>
