<p><p>The differential pressure level transmitter market comprises three main types: straight rod, flange, and screw-type transmitters. Straight rod transmitters feature a simple design, ideal for direct tank level measurement. Flange type transmitters are mounted on tank flanges, providing secure connections for high-pressure applications. Screw-type transmitters utilize screw fittings for easy installation and maintenance, suitable for various industrial settings. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of application suitability, installation ease, and pressure handling capabilities, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
