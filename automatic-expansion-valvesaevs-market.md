<p><p>Automatic Expansion Valves (AEVs) regulate refrigerant flow in HVAC and refrigeration systems, ensuring optimal operation and efficiency. They maintain a constant superheat level, adjusting to varying load conditions. The market for AEVs includes two primary types: those without bypass, which directly manage refrigerant flow without excess bypass channels, and those with bypass, which incorporate a mechanism to reroute refrigerant to enhance system stability. Each type caters to specific system requirements, optimizing performance and energy use.</p></p>
