<p><p>The oxidized polyethylene wax market is categorized into low-density and high-density types, each serving distinct applications. In contrast, high-density oxidized polyethylene wax offers enhanced hardness and higher melting points, making it ideal for applications like plastics, rubber, and masterbatches.
