<p><p>The robotics and automation actuator market comprises three primary types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic actuators. Electric actuators utilize electrical energy to drive motion, offering precise control and efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate movement, favored for their quick action and simplicity in setups requiring moderate force. Hydraulic actuators leverage pressurized fluids, delivering substantial power, ideal for heavy lifting tasks. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driving advancements in automation technologies.</p></p>
