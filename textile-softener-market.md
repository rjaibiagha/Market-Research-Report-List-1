<p><strong>The Textile Softener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The textile softener market comprises three main types: liquid softeners, dryer sheets, and dryer balls. Liquid softeners are added during the wash cycle, enhancing fabric softness and reducing static cling. Dryer sheets, used in the drying process, offer convenience and additional fragrance while also softening clothes. Dryer balls, typically made of wool or rubber, help separate clothes in the dryer, promoting airflow and reducing drying time while naturally softening fabrics without chemicals. Each type presents unique benefits for consumers.</p></p>
