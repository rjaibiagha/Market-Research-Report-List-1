<p><p>The phase rotation meter market is segmented into two main types: rotating phase rotation meters and static phase rotation meters. Rotating phase rotation meters employ mechanical components that visibly indicate the phase sequence through a rotating mechanism, ideal for real-time monitoring in various applications. Both types are essential for ensuring proper phase alignment in electrical systems.</p></p>
