<p><p>Riveting equipment can be categorized into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic riveting tools utilize compressed air, offering high speed and efficiency for assembly tasks. Hydraulic riveters rely on fluid pressure to deliver powerful force, ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring precision. Electric riveting equipment uses electrical power for operation, providing versatility and ease of use in various environments. Each type has distinct advantages, catering to different industrial needs and operational requirements in the riveting process.</p></p>
