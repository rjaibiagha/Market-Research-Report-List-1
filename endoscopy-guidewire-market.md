<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Coiled</li><li>Coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscopy guidewire market is categorized into three main types: monofilament, coiled, and coated. Monofilament guidewires are single-stranded, offering stiffness and support for precise navigation. Coiled guidewires feature a helical design that provides flexibility and reduces the risk of kinking, enhancing maneuverability in complex anatomical paths. Coated guidewires are treated with specialized materials to reduce friction and improve biocompatibility, facilitating smoother passage through tight or challenging areas. Each type serves distinct roles in medical procedures, ensuring optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
