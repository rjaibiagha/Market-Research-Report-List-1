<p><p>Helmet-mounted Head-Up Displays (HUDs) enhance situational awareness for users, primarily in aviation and military applications. The market can be divided into two types: inside the helmet and outside the helmet. Inside helmet systems project information directly onto a visor, providing immediate data while ensuring unobstructed vision. Outside helmet systems, on the other hand, project data onto an external screen or the environment, allowing for a broader field of view but potentially increasing visual clutter. Both types aim to improve user performance and safety.</p></p>
