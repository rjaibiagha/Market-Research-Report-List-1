<p><p>The High Thermal Conductivity Copper Foil market is primarily divided into two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through mechanical processes, offering superior thickness and surface quality, making it suitable for various applications. Electrolytic copper foil, derived from electrochemical deposition, features a finer grain structure and uniform thickness, ideal for high-performance applications like electronics and heat dissipation. Both types are crucial in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics, where thermal management is essential.</p></p>
