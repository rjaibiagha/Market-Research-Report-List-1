<p><p>The urinary drainage catheter market encompasses three primary types: rubber, plastic (PVC), and silicone. Plastic (PVC) catheters are lightweight and have a smooth surface, making them suitable for both short and medium-term use. Silicone catheters are more biocompatible, designed for extended use due to their durability and reduced risk of irritation. Each type caters to varying patient needs and durations, influencing healthcare choices in urinary management.</p></p>
