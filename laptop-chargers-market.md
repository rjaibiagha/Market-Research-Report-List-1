<p><p>The laptop charger market can be divided into two main types: AC and DC chargers. AC chargers are primarily used for charging laptops through standard electrical outlets, converting alternating current from the grid into direct current suitable for laptop batteries. These chargers are essential for indoor use or when connected to power sources. In contrast, DC chargers are designed for use in vehicles or portable power stations, providing direct current that matches the laptop's power requirements, allowing for charging on the go.</p></p>
