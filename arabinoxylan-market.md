<p><p>The Arabinoxylan market is categorized into two main types: water-extractable and alkali-extractable. Alkali-extractable arabinoxylans, on the other hand, require an alkaline solution for extraction and are primarily used in industrial applications, including paper and biofuels, due to their structural benefits. Both types serve distinct functions across various industries, catering to specific formulation needs.</p></p>
