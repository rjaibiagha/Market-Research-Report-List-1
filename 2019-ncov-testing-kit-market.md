<p><strong>The 2019 nCoV Testing Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent PCR</li><li>Colloidal Gold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2019 nCoV testing kit market is primarily divided into two types: Fluorescent PCR and Colloidal Gold assays. Fluorescent PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a highly sensitive technique that detects viral RNA through amplification, enabling precise identification of the virus. In contrast, Colloidal Gold tests are rapid diagnostic kits that use gold nanoparticles to visually indicate the presence of the virus, providing quick results but with lower sensitivity compared to PCR methods. Both play vital roles in COVID-19 diagnostics.</p></p>
