<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion</li><li>Non Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame retardant nonwoven market can be divided into two main types: emulsion and non-emulsion. Emulsion-based flame retardants are typically water-soluble formulations that allow for easy application to nonwoven fabrics, providing a durable fire-resistant property. Conversely, non-emulsion flame retardants often consist of solid additives or chemicals that can be incorporated during the manufacturing process, offering robust fire protection without compromising the material's integrity. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as textiles, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
