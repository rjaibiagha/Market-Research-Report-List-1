<p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market is divided into two primary types: SMD Type and Disc Type. SMD (Surface Mount Device) MOVs are compact, designed for automated assembly on printed circuit boards, offering space-saving advantages and improved reliability. Conversely, Disc Type MOVs feature a larger, disc-shaped design, widely used for overvoltage protection in various applications. Both types serve crucial roles in safeguarding electronic systems, but their selection depends on factors like application, space constraints, and performance requirements.</p></p>
