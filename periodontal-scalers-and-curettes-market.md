<p><p>The periodontal scalers and curettes market is segmented into mechanical and oscillatory types. Mechanical scalers utilize ultrasonic vibrations to efficiently remove plaque and tartar from teeth, offering deep cleaning with minimal effort. Oscillatory devices, on the other hand, use rapid back-and-forth movements to disrupt biofilm and debris, enhancing periodontal treatment effectiveness. Both types are essential in dental practices, providing dentists with versatile tools to maintain oral health and address periodontal conditions effectively.</p></p>
