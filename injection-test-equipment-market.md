<p><strong>The Injection Test Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Injection Test Equipment</li><li>Secondary Injection Test Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injection Test Equipment market is categorized into two main types: Primary and Secondary Injection Test Equipment. Primary Injection Test Equipment is used for testing protective relays and circuit breakers by injecting a high current directly into the device, ensuring its proper operation and response under fault conditions. Secondary Injection Test Equipment, on the other hand, simulates input signals to the protective devices, allowing for testing without the need to interrupt the power supply, ensuring safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
