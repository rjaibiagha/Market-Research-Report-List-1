<p><p>Bioactive Bone Void Fillers are innovative medical products used to fill bone defects, enhance bone regeneration, and promote healing. As they integrate with the surrounding bone, they gradually resorb and are replaced by new bone, making them particularly useful in orthopedic, spinal, and dental surgeries.</p><p>The Bioactive Bone Void Filler Market is projected to grow significantly, with an anticipated CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.
