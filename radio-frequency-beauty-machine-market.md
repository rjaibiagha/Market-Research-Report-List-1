<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Beauty Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Radio Frequency</li><li>Bipolar Radio Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Beauty Machine market is divided into unipolar and bipolar radio frequency types, each serving distinct cosmetic purposes. Unipolar RF utilizes a single electrode, penetrating deeper into the skin, making it effective for body contouring and cellulite reduction. In contrast, bipolar RF uses two electrodes, targeting the superficial layers, making it ideal for facial treatments like skin tightening and wrinkle reduction. Both technologies leverage radio frequency energy to stimulate collagen production, enhancing skin appearance and texture.</p></p>
