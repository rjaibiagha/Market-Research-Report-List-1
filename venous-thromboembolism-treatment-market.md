<p><p>The Venous Thromboembolism (VTE) treatment market is categorized into two main types: Deep Venous Thrombosis (DVT) and Pulmonary Embolism (PE). DVT involves the formation of blood clots in deep veins, primarily in the legs, requiring anticoagulants, thrombolytics, and supportive therapies for management. PE occurs when a clot migrates to the lungs, causing severe complications. Treatment focuses on anticoagulants, interventional procedures, and sometimes surgical options. The market encompasses medications, diagnostics, and technologies addressing these conditions comprehensively.</p></p>
