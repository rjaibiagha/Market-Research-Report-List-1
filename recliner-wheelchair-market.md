<p><p>The recliner wheelchair market comprises two primary types: electric and mechanical. Electric recliner wheelchairs are powered by motors, enabling easy adjustment of the backrest and leg support at the push of a button, providing enhanced comfort for users with limited mobility. Mechanical recliner wheelchairs, on the other hand, rely on manual mechanisms, allowing users or caregivers to adjust the seating position using levers or handles. Both types cater to various user needs, offering improved comfort and support for different medical conditions.</p></p>
