<p><ul><li>HDI PCB (1+N+1)</li><li>HDI PCB (2+N+2)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDI boards, or High-Density Interconnect boards, come in various market types based on their layer configurations. The HDI PCB (1+N+1) structure features one outer layer, an inner layer (N), and one additional outer layer. In contrast, the HDI PCB (2+N+2) design incorporates two outer layers, an inner layer (N), and two additional outer layers. These configurations provide enhanced routing density, improved electrical performance, and miniaturization, making them ideal for advanced electronics applications in industries like telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
